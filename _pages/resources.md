### DrBERT
DrBERT is a French biomedical language model trained on clinical and scientific data to support NLP tasks in the medical domain.  
- Data and model: https://huggingface.co/Dr-BERT  
- Publication (ACL 2023): https://aclanthology.org/2023.acl-long.896/

### BioMistral
BioMistral is a biomedical LLM designed for advanced medical and scientific text generation and understanding.  
- Data and model: https://huggingface.co/BioMistral  
- Publication (ACL 2024): https://aclanthology.org/2024.findings-acl.348/

### DrBenchmark
DrBenchmark is a comprehensive evaluation benchmark for assessing the performance of language models on medical tasks.  
- Benchmark repository: https://github.com/DrBenchmark/DrBenchmark  
- Publication (LREC-COLING 2024): https://aclanthology.org/2024.lrec-main.478/

### CareMedEval
CareMedEval is a benchmark designed to evaluate medical language models on healthcare-related tasks and real-world scenarios.  
- Data and repository: https://github.com/bonzid/CareMedEval  
- Publication (LREC 2026): https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.03441
